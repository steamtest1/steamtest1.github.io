Registration Details & Activities


We are meeting online or in-person on weekends; offering a hybrid model. A total of 6 classes + 2 STEAM challenges + 2 STEAM events are included in the registration fees. The classes are conducted by different STEAM entities and coordinated by parent volunteers. We also have different STEAM events for kids. They learn leadership and team work skills along with science, technology, engineering, art and math logic while working with our community. Students also learn to connect with a community which follows the core values of Jainism.

Tuition for classes are $175. Space is limited. Payment must be completed in order to secure your spot. Registration will be based on a first-come-first serve basis. NO MAKEUPS/REFUNDS. All parents are required attend a mandatory parent meeting on the first day of class. Dates will be announced after registration.
NEW CLASSES & EVENTS FOR THE 2021-2022 YEAR:

  • • STEAM Art workshop
  • • Finance education & Business
  • • STEAM challenges
  • • Mock trial and case study
  • • Robotics
  • • Outdoor events

Member Perks – You pay a low registration fee for expensive and innovative programs. Your family and yourself get to be a part of a community with similar cultural values. We encourage parent volunteering/youth volunteering (see below button for guidelines) and accept sponsors for the STEAM club activities. Classes can change without any prior notice.
